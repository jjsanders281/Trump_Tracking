# Backfill Targets

## What "Backfill Targets" Means
Backfill targets are fixed quotas for how much historical data you add each week/month, so old material is recovered systematically instead of randomly.

## Recommended Baseline
- Phase 1 (first 6 weeks): 300 to 500 high-impact claims.
- Phase 2: 150 claims per month until major gaps are closed.

## Prioritization Order
1. High-impact topics (elections, violence, constitutional claims, foreign policy, public health).
2. Frequently repeated claims.
3. Claims with clear later contradictions.
4. Claims often cited by supporters in current debates.

## Coverage Strategy
- Start at June 16, 2015 and move forward in monthly blocks.
- Build a "minimum viable day" rule: at least one verified entry per day when available.
- Track gaps explicitly (days with no reliable archive material).

## Quality Guardrail
If a monthly target conflicts with evidence quality, reduce volume and preserve verification standards.
