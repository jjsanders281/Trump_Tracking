# Shared Reference: Taxonomy Standards

## Canonical Topics
Use a stable topic set to avoid taxonomy drift:
- Elections
- Healthcare
- Immigration
- Economy
- Foreign Policy
- Justice and Courts
- Governance and Norms
- Media and Information
- Public Safety

## Claim Kinds
- `statement`: descriptive assertion.
- `promise`: future commitment.
- `denial`: rejection of prior claim/event.
- `attack`: accusation about person/group.
- `policy`: statement tied to policy action.

## Impact Score (1-5)
- 1: low-salience or narrow impact.
- 2: notable but limited scope.
- 3: broad public relevance.
- 4: high civic or legal significance.
- 5: major institutional, democratic, or safety impact.
